About Castle Rock Roof Repair

Douglas County's hail damage specialists — locally owned, Colorado tough.

Built for Colorado Weather

Castle Rock Roof Repair was founded over 12 years ago with a simple mission: give Douglas County homeowners a roofing contractor they can trust — especially when the hail hits. We've been through every major storm that's hammered the Front Range, and we'll be here for the next one.

We're not storm chasers from out of state. We're not a franchise. We're a locally owned company right here in Castle Rock. Our crew lives in these neighborhoods. When a storm rolls through, we're assessing our own roofs before we start on yours — that's how local we are.

Colorado roofing is different. At 6,200+ feet, the intense UV radiation breaks down shingles years faster than at sea level. Hail season runs from April through September, and a single storm can damage thousands of roofs in an afternoon. Then winter brings ice dams, heavy snow loads, and freeze-thaw cycles that test every seam and seal. We understand these challenges because we deal with them every single day.

As licensed Colorado contractors and insurance claim specialists, we don't just fix roofs — we fight for our customers. We document damage thoroughly, meet with adjusters on-site, and make sure your claim gets the attention it deserves. That's what sets us apart from the guys who show up after a storm and disappear a month later.
