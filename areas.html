Douglas County & Surrounding Areas

Our home base. We serve every community in Douglas County and beyond.

Castle Rock

Our home base and the heart of Douglas County. Castle Rock homeowners face some of the worst hail in Colorado — we've repaired hundreds of roofs right here in town after major storms. From the Meadows to Founders Village, we know every neighborhood.

Parker

Just north on Hwy 83, Parker's growing neighborhoods need reliable roofing. We serve Parker homeowners with hail damage repair, replacements, and storm restoration — usually same-day response.

Lone Tree

Lone Tree's upscale homes deserve premium roofing. We install impact-resistant architectural shingles and standing seam metal roofing built to handle Colorado's intense UV and hail.

Highlands Ranch

One of Colorado's largest planned communities — and a frequent target for hail. We've completed full neighborhood re-roofs in Highlands Ranch after major storms and are trusted by HOAs throughout the area.

Larkspur

Nestled between Castle Rock and Monument, Larkspur's rural properties and newer developments all need roofing built for altitude. We serve the entire Larkspur area including Perry Park.

Sedalia

Sedalia's rural properties and ranchettes face the full force of Front Range storms. We provide roofing services for homes and agricultural buildings throughout the Sedalia area.

Franktown

East of Castle Rock along Hwy 86, Franktown's horse properties and acreages need durable roofing. We handle everything from residential repairs to barn and outbuilding re-roofs.

Elizabeth

Further east in Elbert County, Elizabeth homeowners count on us for reliable roofing. We make the drive because this community deserves the same quality service as anywhere else we work.

Monument

South along I-25 near the El Paso County line, Monument sits at even higher elevation — meaning more UV exposure and faster shingle degradation. We install roofs rated to withstand it.

Palmer Lake

This charming mountain town south of Monument deals with heavy snow loads and ice dams on top of hail season. We provide ice dam prevention, repairs, and full replacements for Palmer Lake residents.

Perry Park

Perry Park's beautiful wooded setting means tree debris and storm exposure are common roofing threats. We serve the entire Perry Park community with storm damage repair and preventive maintenance.

Castle Pines

Castle Pines' premium homes need premium roofing. We work with HOAs and individual homeowners to install impact-resistant roofing systems that protect property values and stand up to Colorado weather.

Roxborough

Tucked against the foothills near Roxborough State Park, this community faces unique wind and hail exposure. We provide roofing services throughout the Roxborough area including Roxborough Park and Ravenna.
