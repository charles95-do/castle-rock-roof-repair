Roofing Services in Castle Rock, CO

From hail damage repair to full replacements — we handle every roofing challenge Colorado weather throws at your home.

Hail Damage Repair

Colorado's hail season (April–September) is brutal. We assess the damage, document everything for your insurance claim, and restore your roof fast.

Roof Replacement

Full tear-off and replacement with impact-resistant shingles built for Colorado's altitude. Architectural shingles, metal roofing, and more.

Storm Damage Restoration

Wind, hail, and fallen tree damage. Emergency tarping, full restoration, and we handle the insurance claims process from start to finish.

Roof Inspection

Insurance claims, home purchases, or annual checkups. Detailed report with photos — especially critical after hail season in Douglas County.

Ice Dam Prevention & Repair

Colorado winters create dangerous ice dams. We install proper ventilation, ice shields, and repair damage caused by ice buildup.

Commercial Roofing

Flat roofs, TPO, EPDM, and modified bitumen for commercial properties along I-25 corridor. Maintenance contracts available.
