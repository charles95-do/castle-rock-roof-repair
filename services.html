Our Roofing Services

Everything your roof needs to survive Colorado's hail, UV, ice, and wind — from emergency repair to full replacement.

Hail Damage Repair

Colorado's Front Range is one of the most hail-prone regions in the entire country. From April through September, severe hailstorms can leave your roof riddled with dents, cracked shingles, and compromised seals — damage that often isn't visible from the ground. Our team specializes in identifying hail damage, documenting it thoroughly for your insurance company, and restoring your roof to pre-storm condition. We've handled hundreds of hail claims across Douglas County and know exactly what adjusters look for.

  • Comprehensive hail damage assessment (free after any storm)
  • Full photo documentation for insurance claims
  • Direct coordination with your insurance adjuster
  • Impact-resistant shingle upgrades (Class 4 rated)
  • Damaged flashing, vent, and gutter repair
  • Soft metal damage inspection (gutters, vents, AC units)

Roof Replacement

At 6,200+ feet of elevation, Castle Rock roofs endure significantly more UV radiation than lower-altitude areas — accelerating shingle degradation by years. When repairs aren't enough, we do full tear-off and replacement with materials specifically rated for Colorado's high-altitude conditions. Most residential roofs are completed in 1–3 days with minimal disruption.

  • Full tear-off and disposal of old roofing
  • Impact-resistant architectural shingle installation (Class 4)
  • Standing seam and corrugated metal roofing
  • Synthetic underlayment and ice/water shield
  • New drip edge, ridge vent, and flashing
  • UV-stabilized materials rated for high altitude
  • Manufacturer warranty + workmanship guarantee

Storm Damage Restoration

Colorado storms bring a devastating combination of high winds, large hail, and heavy rain — sometimes all in the same afternoon. We handle the full restoration process from the moment the storm passes: emergency tarping to prevent interior damage, thorough assessment, full insurance documentation, and complete roof restoration. We're local, so we're here before the storm chasers even arrive.

  • Emergency tarping and board-up (same-day)
  • Wind, hail, and impact damage assessment
  • Insurance claim assistance — we meet with your adjuster on-site
  • Full photo and video documentation for your claim
  • Tree damage removal and structural repair
  • Complete storm damage restoration

Roof Inspection (Insurance Claims)

After every hail season, we recommend a professional inspection — even if your roof looks fine from the ground. Hail damage is often subtle: small dents in shingles, cracked granule surfaces, and compromised seals that lead to leaks months later. We provide thorough inspections for insurance claims, home purchases, and preventive maintenance, with a detailed written report you can share with your insurer or real estate agent.

  • Post-storm hail damage inspections (free)
  • Pre-purchase home buyer inspections
  • Insurance claim inspections with adjuster coordination
  • Annual maintenance inspections
  • Detailed report with photos and recommendations
  • Honest assessment — we won't sell you a roof you don't need

Ice Dam Prevention & Repair

Castle Rock's cold winters and frequent freeze-thaw cycles create the perfect conditions for ice dams — ridges of ice that form at your roof's edge and trap melting snow, forcing water under your shingles and into your home. We address both the symptoms and the root cause with proper ventilation, insulation improvements, and ice shield installation.

  • Ice dam removal (safe, damage-free methods)
  • Ice and water shield membrane installation
  • Attic ventilation assessment and improvement
  • Insulation evaluation to prevent heat loss
  • Gutter de-icing solutions
  • Interior water damage repair from ice dam leaks
